FAQ

Why are my plots empty?

Ensure you selected the correct experiment folder and action, and confirm that the PCAP files were captured for that action. If packet counts are zero, re-check the Wi-Fi profile settings.

Why does the CSI array have fewer dimensions than expected?

1SS captures do not include the Tx axis. The CSI tensor becomes [time, subcarrier, rx] instead of [time, subcarrier, rx, tx].

Why is the depth camera feed missing?

The depth camera requires a local webserver for streaming. Verify that the server is running, the configured URL is reachable, and (on macOS) that the D455 is visible inside the VM hosting the RealSense drivers.

Why are voice prompts not playing?

Confirm that the voice profile has spoken prompts enabled and that gTTS plus audio playback are installed and accessible on your system.

Why do I see time drift between sessions?

Re-sync the time profile before starting a new experiment and verify the configured time server is reachable from the host machine.
